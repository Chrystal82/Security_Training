Welcome to our Security Awareness Training Quiz! In today's digital age, where cyber threats are ever-present, it's essential to stay informed and vigilant to protect our organization's data and assets. This quiz will cover key topics to enhance your understanding of security practices, focusing on:

  1. Email Security: Recognizing phishing attempts, understanding email encryption, and practicing safe email habits to mitigate risks.
  2. Internet Safety: Navigating the internet securely, identifying malicious websites, and understanding the importance of HTTPS and secure browsing
  3. Personalized Threats: Being aware of targeted attacks such as spear phishing and social engineering, and learning how to spot and respond to personalized threats.
  4. Malware Awareness: Understanding different types of malware, recognizing signs of infection, and Implementing preventive measures to safeguard against malware attacks.
  5. Passwords: Grasping the importance of strong, unique passwords, implementing password management best practices, and understanding the role of passwords in overall security hygiene.

By mastering these topics, you'll not only protect yourself but also contribute to our organization's overall security posture. Let's embark on this learning journey together to fortify our defenses against cyber threats. Let's begin the quiz!
